Get Started

To get started, just send your first idea to ideas@dailyidea.com with the subject "Sign Up".

The format is:

  1. First, a short, one-line description of the idea
  2. (a blank row)
  3. Then as much detail or elaboration on the idea as you would like to include

For example: 

Longer Lasting Cell Phone Batteries

Cell phones always inconveniently run out of batteries by 6pm! I bet people would love batteries that last a week!
